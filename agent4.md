# Agent 4

There is nothing quite as soothing as the gentle rumble of a cat purring contentedly in your lap. The soft vibrations seem to melt away stress, and when you run your fingers through their fur and feel the delicate whiskers brush against your hand, it creates a moment of pure, quiet connection. Scientists believe that the frequency of a cat's purr may even promote healing, making these furry companions not just a source of comfort but tiny, rumbling therapists curled up beside you.
